Pastor's Profile

Rev. Eunice Ndirangu is a licenced pastor. She is currently the presiding Senior pastor of Kenyan Global Church (KGC). Rev. Eunice has been a member of KGC for over eight years, but she was appointed Senior pastor of KGC two years ago. The pastor deals with the Spiritual matters of the church.

Become a member

How Join Kenyan Global Church

To be a member of KGC, one needs to fill an Application form normally given by Church Secretary. The prospective member fills the Application form with the personal information and returns the form to the Church secretary for filing. It is paramount that one completes this form upon joining the church.

KGC is a charitable organization. Therefore, a person should feel free to give donations either once or monthly or every Sunday or join us as the church conducts fundraising events for the Church Building Fund. The donor will be given a receipt for your Tax Returns purposes.

Celebrate 10 years anniversary with us

Sunday Worship Services

KGC holds its services every Sunday from 12:00 pm to 1:30 pm and is followed by the sharing of snacks offered by the church members. This creates a forum to socialize with each another and to share information.
